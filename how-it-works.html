How It Works

Rock Solid Tips is designed to be simple, direct, and honest — for both drivers and customers.

For Customers

  1. Scan the driver’s QR code.
  2. Choose a tip amount.
  3. Your tip goes directly to the driver.

No accounts to create. No apps to download. No confusion.

For Drivers

  1. Share your Rock Solid Tips QR code with customers.
  2. Customers tip you directly.
  3. You receive and control your tips.

You decide how tips are received and managed using your chosen payment method.

What Makes It Different

Rock Solid Tips removes middlemen, delays, and split payouts. Tips are not bundled, held, or rerouted through platforms.

It’s a direct connection between the person who did the work and the person who wants to say thank you.

Why It Works

When tipping is simple and transparent, everyone wins. Customers know where their money goes, and drivers keep control of what they earned.

That’s the entire system. No extras. No tricks.
