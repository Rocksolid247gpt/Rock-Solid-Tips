Rock Solid Tips

Rock Solid Gratitude

Rock Solid Tips provides QR codes and tipping tools designed to help tip-earning workers receive tips directly.

The platform is built for gig drivers, waitresses, taxi drivers, and anyone working in service who earns tips.

Customers simply scan the QR code and tip the worker directly.

No unnecessary layers.
No confusion about where the tip goes.

Just a direct connection between the customer and the worker.

Rock Solid Tips is about respect for the people doing the work and making sure their effort is recognized.

Over time, the vision for Rock Solid Tips is to grow into something even bigger, with future programs that could support tip-earning workers through things like emergency assistance and safety resources.
