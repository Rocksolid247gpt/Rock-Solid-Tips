About Rock Solid Tips

Rock Solid Tips was created by an independent driver.

I work hard. I do my best. And at the end of the day, drivers deserve their tips.

We earned them — and we should control them. How they’re paid. When they’re paid. And who they’re paid to.

Direct tipping removes middlemen, delays, and tips being split up, held back, or paid out in pieces. What a customer gives should go directly to the driver.

Rock Solid Tips was built by drivers, for drivers.

Powered by truth.

This isn’t just a platform — it’s peace of mind.

Why It Matters

Tipping is what makes this work. It’s how customers say thank you, and how drivers stay motivated, present, and reliable.

Without customers and without tips, the work doesn’t function the way it’s meant to. And without drivers, the entire system falls apart.

Rock Solid Tips exists to keep tipping honest, direct, and in the hands of the people who earned it.
