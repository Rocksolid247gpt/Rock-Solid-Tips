Rock Solid Tips

Rock Solid Gratitude

Rock Solid Tips was created to make tipping simple, direct, and visible. It gives tip-earning workers a better way to receive support from customers without the confusion, delays, or clutter.

The platform is built for gig drivers, waitresses, taxi drivers, service workers, and anyone else who earns tips through real work and real effort.

Customers scan a QR code and tip the worker directly. No middleman. No guesswork. No confusion about where the tip goes.

Rock Solid Tips is about respect for the people doing the work. It is built to support direct connection between customer and worker through simple tools that are easy to use and easy to understand.

This platform was built from vision, determination, and grit. The mission is bigger than tipping alone. It is about recognition, support, and building something strong for the people out there earning it every day.
