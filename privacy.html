Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what Rock Solid Tips does — and does not — collect.

Age Requirement

Rock Solid Tips is intended for use by individuals who are 21 years of age or older.

This requirement exists because many drivers using Rock Solid Tips deliver age-restricted items, including alcohol, and must meet legal age requirements to perform those services.

What We Collect

Rock Solid Tips does not collect personal information from customers who scan a QR code to tip a driver.

We do not collect names, payment details, addresses, or banking information.

Payments

Tips and payments are processed entirely through third-party payment services chosen by individual drivers.

Rock Solid Tips does not process, store, or have access to payment information.

Information You Provide

If you contact Rock Solid Tips directly (for example, by email), we may receive the information you choose to share. That information is used only to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies & Tracking

Rock Solid Tips does not use tracking cookies, behavioral advertising, or sell user data.

Basic hosting or analytics tools provided by third-party services may collect anonymous technical data such as browser type or page visits.

Third-Party Services

Links or QR codes on Rock Solid Tips may direct you to third-party payment providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Rock Solid Tips constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
